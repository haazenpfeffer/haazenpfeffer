About

Hello, my name is Kyle Haas, and you've stumbled upon my humble website. I spend my weekends analyzing peanut butter as a QA Lab Technician and my weekdays providing computer tech support, building custom gaming rigs, gaming, and experimenting with technology.

Although I have a BS in Biology, my fascination with life science has only been matched by my affinity for technology. I've always had a distinct knack for computers; I'm completely self-taught and have been experimenting with computers since before I could even read. I also have a love of philosophy, debate, and art.

I've undertaken many careers as a young adult, and although I'm currently working in a QA lab, I have also spent time providing emergency first aid as a certfied Emergency Medical Technician.

Throughout all of my life, my troubleshooting skills and charisma have always been some of my strongest attributes. I've also studied in Europe, and can speak German casually. I've always been a natural leader and I enjoy speaking to groups.

I'm a proud graduate of Saint Xavier Highschool and Bellarmine University--both here in my hometown of Louisville, Kentucky. At Saint X, I was a leader of the German Club, Dance Dance Revolution Club, Computer Club, and Computer-Gaming Club. At Bellarmine, I was president of the university Emergency Response Team.

Any questions should be sent to my gmail address, although I check all social media regularly. Online tech advice is always free.
